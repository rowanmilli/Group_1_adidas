Primeblue SST Track Jacket

An iconic track jacket made with recycled materials.

A track jacket like never before. Sure, this may look just like iconic adidas gear from the archive, but this Primeblue SST Track Top symbolizes change. The iconic silhouette is made with Parley Ocean Plastic that's made from upcycled plastic waste collected from ocean shores. It's a statement style that's good for the planet too. Stand up, stand out and join adidas' commitment to help end plastic waste.
